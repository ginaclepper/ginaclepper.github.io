Notes/Lessons Learned

The origin of the Othermill is in the front left corner!

The Bantam software doesn't seem to like a blank gerber file for engraving. I worked around this, but I'm certain there's a better way!

Dogbones trigger a warning about needing a smaller tool. I ignored this warning without issue -- but should I have?

KiCAD doesn't like overlapping paths, and possibly may not like hidden layers that contain overlapping paths

Needed to export DXF with “lines” scheme (I did most recent, 2018) for it to show up as having graphic content in KiCAD
